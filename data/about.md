# ezOS

**ezOS v2 (feature/offline-maps-v5-zlib)**

A C++ firmware and Lua userspace for the LilyGo *T-Deck Plus*. Mesh
networking over LoRa, offline maps, and a declarative UI stack — all in
under 2 MB of flash.
