SND01 "Sine" Sound Pack
========================

Source:       https://snd.dev/
Designer:     Yasuhiro Tsuchiya
Publisher:    Dentsu Inc.
Pack:         SND01_sine (2021-12-24)

Files in this directory are derived from the original 44.1 kHz 16-bit
WAV distribution; they have been resampled to 22.05 kHz 16-bit mono
raw PCM for use on the T-Deck Plus. The two loop assets
(ringtone_loop, progress_loop) are omitted.

The snd.dev terms permit incorporating modified assets into personal,
commercial and open-source projects. Redistribution of the pack in
its original unprocessed form or registering these sounds as
trademarked sound logos is not permitted. A credit link back to
snd.dev is requested; this project displays that credit in the
About screen (Settings -> About).
